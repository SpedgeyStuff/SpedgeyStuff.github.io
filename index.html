Daniel McKinley

Aspiring software developer, artist and enthusiastic gamer.

I'm a 21-year-old CompSci student in London. Throughout my course, I've been - and will continue to be - developing various software based on assignments and personal interest. Outside of my studies, I mostly play video games or draw. I've drawn for as long as I can remember, and have recently started making use of my skills through digital drawing. Much of my work will be displayed on this website. Thanks for visiting!
